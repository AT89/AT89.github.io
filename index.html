OneAux

So you're at a party, funciton or roadtrip but everyone wants to play their song and is fighting over the Aux cord?

Easy solution, OneAux works with Spotify to provide a dynamic playlist that the 'host' creates and users can join the playlist, add songs from Spotify and then vote songs up and down to arrange the playlist order

Technologies used were Spotify API, OneAuxAPI(ours),Spotify Angular Helper, AngularJS, Ruby on Rails backend. This was our Group project at General Assembly. Had the pleasure of working with James Graham and Tyson Greene.
